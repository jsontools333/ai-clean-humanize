Manual polishing tips

Final touches that no automated tool can fully replace.

  • tip Replace most em-dashes (—) with periods or commas.
  • tip Break long, perfectly-balanced sentences into shorter ones.
  • tip Remove generic openers like "it is important to note" or "in today's world."
  • tip Add a concrete example wherever the text sounds vague.
  • tip Vary paragraph length. Uniformity is the loudest AI tell.

Note: rewriting does not reliably defeat AI detectors, and detectors themselves are unreliable. Use this as an editing aid.
